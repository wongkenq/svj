Health Education

Judo requires development of your body. It promotes healthy training and living as a life-long endeavor.

Grit Development

Everyone's grittiness may start out at different levels. But it can be developed. We want to help build a strong foundation that will last a lifetime.

Self Defense

Judo comes from the original unarmed combat systems of Feudal Japan. Judo remains an efficient and practical self defense system and has evolved to use the sports ethic and mentality to give its practitioners live practical application.

Competition

Judo offers competitions where they can apply their training and get feedback on how to develop to the next level. It's also an Olympic Sport and allows its students to reach for Olympic dreams of Gold.

Community

Judo is a social activity. No one can train without partners and so we grow together as a team. Joining Judo is joining a group of like-minded and motivated friends who can help each other achieve new goals.

Tradition

Judo was founded in 1882 but comes from the much older martial arts culture of ancient Japan. Those ideals and traditions endure today.

Technology

Using the tools of today we can help build the success of tomorrow.
